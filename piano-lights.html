Piano Lights - [Link to Github]

This project involved connecting a LED strip to a MIDI keyboard, so that each key press would shoot a beam of light down the LED strip. The color of the beam of light is determined by the specific key pressed on the keyboard; the 88 keys are mapped to a rainbow of colors from blue (low) to red (middle) to green (high).
